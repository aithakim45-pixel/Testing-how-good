Why Choose GEO 5?

Modular Architecture

Over 30 specialized programs for slope stability, excavation design, retaining walls, and foundation analysis that seamlessly transfer data.

Global Standards

Fully compliant with international engineering practices, supporting Eurocode 7 (EN 1997-1), LRFD (AASHTO), and numerous national annexes.

BIM Integration

Built for modern workflows. Effortlessly export data to common BIM formats (like IFC and LandXML) to share with third-party architectural software.

Comprehensive Reports

Generate clear, customizable structural analysis reports with editable graphical outputs that automatically update when input data changes.
